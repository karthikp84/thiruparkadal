Thiruparkadal — The Ocean of Milk

A short, crisp story to pair with your Golu image

Story

In the heart of the cosmos lies Thiruparkadal, the Ocean of Milk. Lord Vishnu reclines on the serpent Adiseshan, with Goddess Lakshmi by his side, surrounded by celestial beings.

From this radiant ocean came the treasures of the world when gods and demons once churned its depths. It is the wellspring of life and balance — whenever dharma weakens, Vishnu rises from Thiruparkadal in a new form to restore harmony. This is not merely an ocean; it is the cosmic bedrock of protection and grace.
